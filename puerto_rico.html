Info about Puerto Rico:

Puerto Rico is spanish for rich port, it is a U.S territory located northeast of the Carribean Sea. It is made up of one giant island and several smaller ones surrounding it.It is known for it's unique creole Hispanic culture, and it's language combing African, and European elements. It became a U.S territory in 1898 afterthe Spanish-American War.

Puerto Rico offers it's visitors a wide variety of places to go..

Puerto Rico has so many things to offer to its visitors these include:

  • Beaches to visit
  • Hiking tours
  • Kayaking
  • Food tours
  • Snorkling lessons, and guided tours
  • Surf lessons
  • And much much more!

It's fantastic scenery, resorts, and activities are what make it a popular travel location every year!
